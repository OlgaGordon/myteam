2015 - 2016 Season

Is 2016 the year Luxury invests in digital? New

Many signs point to yes. LVMH recently hired a Chief Digital Officer (CDO) for the first time. Previously, eCommerce sat under the CMO and did not have a formal seat at the C-suite table. Additionally, as sales growth in China cools, luxury brands will need to explore other channels for growth.

Digital is a natural Luxury customers over-index for digital saavy and are trained to shop on their desktops and more frequently on mobile. The ability to purchase luxury goods on a mobile platform may play into the implusive behavior of their customers. For example, Bentley counts that almost 50% of their Chinese customers purchase a car on a whim.
